Habit Dashboard Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Habit Dashboard is designed to work primarily with local data stored on your device.

What data the app stores
- Habit titles and habit settings you create.
- Completion history, skips, slips, milestones, and notes.
- Reminder preferences and onboarding/theme preferences.
- Backup files that you explicitly export.

How data is used
- Your data is used only to power habit tracking features, analytics, reminders, and backup/restore flows inside the app.
- The app does not require account creation.
- The app does not sell personal data.

Notifications
- If you enable reminders, notification settings and reminder content are stored locally so the app can schedule notifications on your device.

Backups
- If you export a backup file or copy backup data to the clipboard, that exported data is under your control.
- Imported backups replace or merge local app data according to the restore flow you choose.

Third-party services
- The app may rely on platform services required by Flutter or your operating system for notifications, file picking, and local storage.
- The app is not designed to upload habit data to a remote server.

Your control
- You can edit, archive, delete, export, import, or reset your data from inside the app.
- You can disable reminders at any time.

Contact
For support or privacy questions, use: support@habitdashboard.app
